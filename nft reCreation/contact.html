Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform, NFTs, and more.

What is an NFT?

NFT stands for Non-Fungible Token. It's a digital asset that represents ownership of a unique item or piece of content on the blockchain. Unlike cryptocurrencies such as Bitcoin, NFTs cannot be exchanged on a one-to-one basis because each one is distinct and has a specific value based on its uniqueness.

How do I buy an NFT on NFTMAK?

To buy an NFT on our platform, you'll need to create an account, connect your digital wallet (like MetaMask), browse our collections, and place a bid or make a direct purchase. After a successful transaction, the NFT will be transferred to your wallet and recorded on the blockchain.

How can I sell my own NFT?

To sell your NFT, you'll need to register as a creator, connect your wallet, upload your digital art or content, set your price or auction terms, and mint your NFT. Our platform will guide you through each step of the process to ensure your creation is properly showcased and available for purchase.

What fees are associated with transactions?

NFTMAK charges a 2.5% platform fee on successful sales. Additionally, there are gas fees for blockchain transactions, which vary based on network congestion. Creators also have the option to set royalty percentages for secondary sales of their work.

How do I report a problem with my purchase?

If you experience any issues with your purchase, please contact our support team through the contact form on this page or email support@nftmak.com. Please include transaction details, wallet address, and a description of the problem for faster resolution.
